Support inbox

One careful route.

Use the form below for privacy, export, deletion, and general support requests. Keep the note brief and private; if we need more information, we will ask for it directly.

What to send

  • Your name and email
  • A short subject such as privacy, export, deletion, or support
  • A plain description of the request or question

What to keep private

  • Artwork photos, receipts, certificates, appraisal files, and provenance notes
  • Prices, full collection lists, account passwords, and other sensitive records
  • Anything you do not want to share unless support asks for the minimum needed
