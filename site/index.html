myartcollection.app

Private records for serious hobby collectors

MyArtCollection is a planned iPhone and Android app for people who want a calmer way to document what they own, keep supporting documents close to each artwork, and prepare for insurance or estate conversations without registrar-level software.

The product direction is straightforward: take a photo, review the record yourself, and keep a private collection record that stays grounded in user-confirmed facts.

Built for the record problem, not the appraisal problem

Photo-first intake

Start from artwork photos and then review the details yourself instead of building a spreadsheet from scratch.

Supporting documents

Keep receipts, certificates, appraisals, and provenance notes with the artwork record so the paperwork is easier to find later.

Quietly practical output

Organize records for insurance, moving, estate, and household documentation needs without turning the app into a marketplace.

Trust boundaries

MyArtCollection is being positioned as a private record tool.

  • It does not determine authenticity.
  • It does not provide appraisal-grade valuation.
  • It does not position supporting documents as proof on their own.

Planned launch copy stays factual and reviewable.

  • AI may assist later, but the user confirms the facts.
  • Privacy claims stay bounded to actual product behavior.
  • Backup, reports, and exports should be described only when shipped.

Who this is for

The product is aimed at serious hobby collectors with roughly 10 to 200 artworks who want organized records, better document handling, and a cleaner answer to what they own, where it is, and what records exist.
