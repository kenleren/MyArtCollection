Pricing

Structured launch pricing for collectors

MyArtCollection is planned with a clear free-versus-paid choice. The free tier lowers trial friction. The paid plan is meant for serious hobby collectors who want organized records, supporting documents, and report-oriented workflows without registrar-level pricing.

Free

USD 0

A simple starting point for trying the product direction.

  • Up to 5 artworks in the launch plan
  • Photo-first record setup
  • Manual review and edits
  • Basic collection view

Collector

USD 9/month

Or USD 79/year for collectors who want the full workflow.

  • Unlimited artworks in the launch plan
  • Supporting document organization
  • Report-oriented record workflows
  • Archive and completeness-focused tooling

Why price it this way

Free lowers trial friction

A meaningful free tier lets collectors test whether the workflow fits their collection habits before committing to paid software.

Paid matches the collector job

The paid plan is aimed at people who need more than a handful of records and want documents, reports, and collection completeness to stay organized without moving into professional registrar pricing.

Pricing boundary

This page defines the current launch pricing intent. It does not claim that web checkout, subscriptions, or account billing are live on this site. It also does not imply authenticity review, appraisal services, or cloud backup promises beyond separately shipped product behavior.
