Privacy

Privacy summary for MyArtCollection

MyArtCollection is being built as a private art record app. This page states the current product direction for `myartcollection.app` and should be updated when shipped behavior changes.

What this page says today

  • The app is intended to be local-first by default.
  • Artwork records should remain user-confirmed, not AI-certified.
  • This product should not claim authenticity or appraised value.
  • No website cookies, analytics, trackers, or contact forms are used on this static site.

Planned app privacy posture

Records and documents

The product direction is to help users keep artwork records, receipts, certificates, appraisals, and notes organized in a private record workflow. Supporting documents help document a record, but they are not described here as proof of authenticity.

Storage direction

The app is planned as local-first. Any future backup or off-device service should be described separately and only when the exact shipped behavior is available to users.

What this site does not do
