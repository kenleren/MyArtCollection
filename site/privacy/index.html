Privacy promise

Your collection record should feel private.

Archivale is positioned as a local-first private art-record archive. Public claims stay careful: records are user-confirmed, AI does not certify facts, and beta signup is a manual interest queue, not automatic access.

01

Local-first posture

Artwork records are framed as a private collection workflow first, not a public marketplace profile.

02

No trackers on this site

This public website does not include analytics, cookies, third-party form tools, or account flows.

03

User-confirmed records

Research or AI assistance can support drafts, but the user decides what becomes part of the record.

04

No false certainty

The product does not claim authenticity, attribution certainty, insurance approval, or appraisal-grade valuation.

Current website behavior

Area What happens here
Cookies and analytics None included on this public website.
Beta signup The beta page includes a first-party form UI, but default public Hosting config does not collect submissions yet. When enabled in a separately reviewed deploy, the queue will accept email, optional name, platform interest, optional country or time zone, optional notes, consent version, retention version, source route, client submit timestamp, and a hidden spam field for manual review. The repo handler does not persist raw IP, browser origin, or user-agent in queued records.
Manual approval A beta-interest record does not create an account and does not automatically add anyone to Firebase App Distribution, Google Play testing, Auth, Remote Config, or tester lists.
Retention and deletion When beta collection is enabled in a reviewed deploy, pending beta-interest records are retained for up to 90 days unless Archivale is actively contacting the person. Rejected records are retained for up to 30 days for audit and abuse review. Honeypot spam records are not queued by the repo handler. Deletion requests are routed through support and targeted for manual handling within 30 days.
Accounts No account creation or login is exposed on this site.
Abuse protection caveat The repo-side handler includes validation, required same-origin checks, honeypot handling, duplicate suppression, and short-lived rate-limit hooks that are not written into queued records. Production deployment still needs an approved App Check or reCAPTCHA posture, durable queue/deletion implementation, and deployment review before collecting real submissions.
Privacy owner Archivale. Questions are routed through the support form.
