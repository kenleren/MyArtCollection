Privacy promise

Your collection record should feel private.

Archivale is positioned as a local-first private art-record archive. Public claims stay careful: records are user-confirmed, AI does not certify facts, and future services will be described only when they ship.

01

Local-first posture

Artwork records are framed as a private collection workflow first, not a public marketplace profile.

02

No trackers on this site

The static public website does not include analytics, cookies, third-party form tools, or account flows.

03

User-confirmed records

Research or AI assistance can support drafts, but the user decides what becomes part of the record.

04

No false certainty

The product does not claim authenticity, attribution certainty, insurance approval, or appraisal-grade valuation.
