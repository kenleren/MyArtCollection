# North Star

## Product Position

Archivale is a private AI-assisted art inventory for serious hobby collectors.

It helps collectors turn photos, receipts, certificates, appraisals, and scattered notes into clean, private, exportable artwork records.

## One-Sentence Promise

Take a photo, confirm the details, and keep every artwork record and document privately organized in your own Google account.

## North Star Outcome

A collector can open the app at any time and produce a credible, organized, exportable record of their collection without wondering whether the data is trustworthy or trapped.

## North Star Metric

Verified artworks per paid account within 30 days.

## Supporting Metrics

- Time to first verified artwork
- Percent of artworks with at least one supporting document
- Percent of paid accounts generating a PDF report or full export
- AI suggestion acceptance and edit rate by field
- Thirty-day paid retention
- Report/export generation rate

## Target User

Primary:

- Serious hobby collector with 10 to 200 works
- Buys from galleries, artists, fairs, auctions, or online platforms
- Has a real but self-managed collection
- Cares about privacy, insurance, provenance, and organization
- Will pay for calm, premium utility

Secondary:

- Household co-manager, spouse, family member, or heir documenting a collection
- Collector preparing insurance, estate, moving, or appraisal records

Avoid initially:

- Museums
- Galleries
- Advisors and family offices
- Professional appraisers
- Speculative investors seeking valuation or market signals

## Product Principles

- Private by default
- Local-first, with optional encrypted backup
- AI suggests; the user confirms
- User-confirmed facts always outrank AI output
- Export must be clear and accessible
- No authenticity, attribution, or appraisal certainty
- Premium utility over flashy novelty

## MVP Definition

The MVP is successful when a new user can:

1. Add an artwork from a photo.
2. Receive a structured AI-assisted draft.
3. Confirm or edit the record.
4. Attach supporting documents.
5. See record completeness and provenance.
6. Generate an insurance-ready PDF.
7. Export their archive without ambiguity.

## Phase

Current phase: Prototype to MVP planning.

The repository is currently greenfield. No implementation should start before the initial product, architecture, privacy, and task breakdown documents are accepted.
